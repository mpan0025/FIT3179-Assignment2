World population in 2020

Population has always been an issue of concern. The Earth's resources are finite, but the population keeps growing. The following visualisation shows the world population in 2020

Map of world's Population

This map shows the population for each Country in 2020. The shade of purple indicates the population size. From the map, we can know that China has the largest population in the world.

Top 10 country with largest population

This chart shows the yearly change of the population for top 10 Countries. From the chart, we can know that Japan has the negative yearly change of the population from 2019 to 2020.

Share of population in the world

This chart shows the share of the population in the world for each Countries. The size of the pie indicates the share of population. From the chart, we can know that China and India has the highest share of population in 2020.

Population density and Land area for each country

This chart shows the population density and land area for each country. The overall trend in this chart is decreasing. Population density is strongly related to land area, and countries with large land areas are generally less densely populated than countries with small populations. While total population also has a strong relationship with population density, this chart shows that land area has more of an effect on population density than total population.
